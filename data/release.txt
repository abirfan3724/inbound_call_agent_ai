### **Pre-Tenancy Services**: 
 
- **Free Advertisement of Rental Property** 
- **Tenant Screening and Qualification**: Ensures all tenants are screened for identity, legal status, 
credit worthiness, 
and income. 
- **House Viewing Assistance**: Facilitation of property viewings through the RealEzy app. If landlords 
are unavailable, 
RealEzy can assist with viewings for a reasonable fee. 
- **Fair and Secure Tenancy Agreement**: Uses a regulatory-approved tenancy agreement that 
incorporates all parties 
(landlord, tenant, RealEzy, and Great American Insurance Company).

tenancy agreement. 
 
### **During Tenancy Services**: 
 
- **Monthly Rent Collection and Rent Reminders**: RealEzy collects rent and sends reminders to 
tenants. 
- **Monthly Statement of Account**: Landlords receive monthly statements of rent payments. 
- **Late Payment Interest Enforcement**: RealEzy enforces late payment interest. 
- **Rental Debt Retrieval Service**: Assistance with rental debt retrieval if required. 
- **Maintenance & Repair Coordination Support**: RealEzy liaises with tenants on behalf of the 
landlord. 
- **Dispute Resolution Management Support**: Helps manage disputes between landlords and 
tenants. 
- **Tenancy Renewal Support**: Assistance with the renewal of tenancy agreements. 
- **Eviction Process Support**: RealEzy provides support during the eviction process if needed.

## Category: General 
 
 
**Q: What is RealEzy?** 
A: RealEzy is Singapore’s first Do-It-Yourself, smartphone and web-based rental property platform that 
connects landlords and tenants directly and safely. It allows free listing of rental properties, including 
rooms, HDB flats, condominiums, apartments, executive condominiums, and landed houses. 
 
 
**Q: How does RealEzy work?** 
A: RealEzy enables landlords to list rental properties for free, and tenants can browse and make offers 
directly. Both parties can communicate securely through the platform. Tenant profiles are verified for 
identity, legal status, creditworthiness, and income. 
 
 
**Q: What types of properties can I list on RealEzy?** 
A: You can list a wide range of properties, including rooms, HDB flats, condominiums, apartments, 
executive condominiums, and landed houses.
